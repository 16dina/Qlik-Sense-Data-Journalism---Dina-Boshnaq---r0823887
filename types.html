Airbnb Types

Entire houses or only rooms?

View Data

Airbnbs are different types, they can be entire houses, private rooms, shared rooms, or a part of a hotel. An interesting fact that was discovered is that the majority of Airbnbs are indeed entire homes both overall and in each city. This can definitely lead to disruption in the housing market as it becomes harder and harder for people to find suitable houses in Belgium, especially in the recent years. The following pie chart illustrates this finding as it shows the percentage of Airbnbs in each city, and if you click on one or choose from the filter, you can see the percentage for each Airbnb type.

Now that we've established the prevalence of entire homes among Airbnb listings and its potential impact on housing availability, let's delve deeper into the dynamics of the Airbnb market. Understanding the distribution of listings among hosts and the concentration of properties in the hands of a few is crucial for policymakers seeking to regulate this industry effectively.
