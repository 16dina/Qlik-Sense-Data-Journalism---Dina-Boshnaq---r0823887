Airbnb Prices

If we look at the average Airbnb price (for a 1 night stay) it's €132.5 but this is for all 3 cities. The average differs from city to city. Choose a city to check out the differences in average price.

If we were to focus on the overall average price which is €132.5 and put ourselves in a host's shoes. For the host, assuming they have the Airbnb booked for the entire month of August, then they make (132.5x31) €4107.5 which is way more than your typical rent price if it were to be rented out to a family/individual as a place of residence. For the host, this is more beneficial and brings in more revenue. This might make property owners who were initially thinking of selling/renting their property, to prefer taking their property off the market and turning it into an Airbnb.

The average price is also generally higher for Airbnbs that are entire houses than other types of Airbnbs.
